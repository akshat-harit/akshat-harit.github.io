Mango Cake

Made mango mousse/no-bake cheese cake. Mixed two recipes. Sponge cake from https://tatyanaseverydayfood.com/mango-mousse-cake/ and mousse/jelly layer from https://www.recipetineats.com/no-bake-mango-cheesecake/ Ingredients are eggs, sugar, vanilla, flour, baking powder, mango puree, gelatin, cream cheese, caster sugar, heavy cream, and lemon juice. Contains gelatin so not suitable for lacto-ova vegetarians. It does look visually stunning but I am a bad photographer. Check the linked recipe blogs for better pictures. Blondies

Made blondies from the recipe https://smittenkitchen.com/2006/11/blondies/ Added chocolate chips, dried blueberries and rum. Used the suggestion for adding alcohol, but I didn’t have whisky or bourbon, so added rum. I basically keep a bottle of alcohol for baking projects and didn’t realize that rum would be much stronger than the suggestions so these did taste strongly of that flavor. I really liked them!
